🌟 Starved

"No one should sleep hungry"

🎯 Pitch

Our game starved follows a girl taylor as she struggls toscavange food in her poverty stricken city. Players follow her through her struggle to find food and also learn about the nuances of this issue: how hunger can affect the decesions people make, oppritunities open to them, and how they are forced to lead their life.

🌍 Theme Implementation

The theme we choose was Outgrow Hunger. Throughout the creation of ths game our 2 main goals were to make palyers more aware about th larger scale causes and effects of starvation and also to inspire them to help resolve this issue. This was accomplished mainly through our game path. As players follow taylor on her scavangging journey they see(through her interactions with people) how hunger is truly an issue larger than the person struggling from it and stems from weak systems. And at the end of our game we have a page dedicated to representing oranizations actively working to rsolve this issue to prompt usrs to help make a difference.

🧠 Why We Built This

We built this game because it was brought to our attention that hunger is a severe national issue that is causing people to struggle. We wanted to create a game that raises awareness towards the issue and teaches people how they can help those in this situation.
